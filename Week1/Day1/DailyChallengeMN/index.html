Information

Antman is one of the avengers.
His superpower is the ability to reduce his size to that of an ant.
Ant-Man's first appearance was in Tales to Astonish #35 (September 1962).
The character has appeared in several films based on the Marvel character, such as:

  1. Ant-Man(2015)
  2. Captain America: Civil War (2016)
  3. Ant-Man and the Wasp (2018)
  4. Avengers Endgame (2019)
